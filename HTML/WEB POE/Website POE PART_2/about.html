About Us

Sweet Cravings Bakery was founded in January 2019 by pastry chef Kayla R. Naidoo. Inspired by her grandmother’s love for baking, Kayla turned her home-based cake hobby into a full-time business that has since become a local favourite in Durban North, KwaZulu-Natal.

Our bakery is best known for:

  • Custom-designed cakes for all occasions
  • Freshly baked artisanal breads
  • Delicious pastries and sweet treats

Vision: To become Durban’s go-to bakery for quality, creativity, and exceptional customer service.

Target Audience: Local residents, event planners, and corporate buyers looking for unique, high-quality baked goods.
