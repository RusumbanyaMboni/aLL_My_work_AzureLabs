Welcome to Sweet Cravings Bakery

Founded in Durban North in 2019 by pastry chef Kayla R. Naidoo, Sweet Cravings Bakery has been delighting customers with custom cakes, freshly baked breads, and artisanal pastries. Inspired by family tradition and driven by creativity, every product is made with love and care.

Our Mission: To bring joy through delicious, handcrafted baked goods made with the freshest ingredients.

Explore our menu and place your custom order today — whether it’s a birthday, wedding, or just a sweet craving, we’ve got you covered!
